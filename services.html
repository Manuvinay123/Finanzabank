    Startup loan

    This loan helps new entrepreneurs to get a good kick start in their business. They are allowed to avail an amount of rs 1000000. The loan will only be granted after the lender submits the complete plan of the business and list of assets he will be purchasing and its depreciation percentage. After considering all the factors if our bank management feels that the business has a positive probability to being a successful business the loan amount will be sanctioned. If the business goes under loss and the lender fails to pay back the lended amount the assets, shares and the company will be sold by the bank in order to recover the money.
